South Elemantry

This is the the school of Perkiomen Valley.

The Home & School Association at South is made up of parent volunteers, teachers and staff who work closely with the principal to provide activities and experiences that enhance our children’s education. Our fundraising efforts and volunteer time support programs such as educational field trips, assemblies, school reading programs, Invention Convention, special teacher requests and school-wide events both during and after school hours.

School Debate competition
Won first prize in inter school debate competition.

Debating in school is a sure-shot way of making your child an accomplished orator. The skills that a child picks up while debating will be of great help to him not just in his career but also in his everyday interactions. So encourage him to debate often and watch as he grows up to be a confident and coherent adult.

Library of South Elemantry

This is the library of South Elemantry where kids come and read books for pleasure.

All students will have regular library class once every other cycle. In addition, we have an “open door policy,” which means that students are welcome to come and trade library books any day or every day. There is no need to wait for the regular library class.
